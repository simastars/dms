E - ATTENDANCE

The primary objective of e-Attendance program is to enter time and data of students attending classes,because it makes taking attendance simplier for the faculty members.

THE ONLINE ATTENDANCE SHEET

Say goodbye the paper-based time sheets! Due to the e-Attendance you can finally forget about the paper-based time sheets and the problems involved in it. The program is available from any part of the world, so it is possible to save time and effort with avoiding collecting paper-based time-sheets and entering their data. It is a common surface, but students have unique USNs and they can record their attendances and the claims of leave.

OBJECTIVE

Department Management System deals with all kind of student and faculty details, academic related reports, course details, curriculum, batch details and other resource related details too. It tracks all the details of a student from the day one to the end of his course like tracking of attendance, progress in the course, curriculum details, exam details, project or any other assignment details, internal assessment results etc.
